(Brief) Life Story

I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, 32 years ago, in a middle-class neighbourhood in the suburbs. I lived in Buenos Aires, in maaany other neighbourhoods (when I left my parents house, I kept moving in and out), for almost the rest of my life in Argentina.
I was an English teacher for 8+ years and I can say it was one of the most rewarding endeavours I've undergone, but I felt it no longer fit my desired way of living.
At 31, I decided to live in Amsterdam for a year and it was an amazing experience, where I met people from lots of countries around the world. This served as a trigger for me, and I can't stress this enough: I need to go on travelling and expanding my perspective(s) on life and others.
Apart from people and places, I also met a new passion: coding, thanks to a new but very close friend of mine, who is an IT Engineer and introduced me to this world by the end of 2019.
And here I am, almost one year since then, every day feeling more and more confident and acquiring more and more experience by the minute. ;)
